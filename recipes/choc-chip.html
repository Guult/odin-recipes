Gooey Chocolate Chip Cookies

If you are a fan of Penn Station's chocolate chip cookies, with huge chunks of chocolate chips, then you'll love this recipe. This recipe uses dark chocolate and semi sweet chocolate chips to give it that perfect balance of sweet and bitter. The taste is of a rich buttery cookie with solid hits of bitter dark chocolate. If dark chocolate isn't your favorite don't be put off as the sugar in the cookie balances this out perfectly.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/3C of all purpose flour
  • 1tsp basking soda
  • 1Tbsp corn starch
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4C unsalted butter, melted and cooled just until opaque
  • 1C brown sugar
  • 1/2C granulated sugar
  • 1 egg, room temperature
  • 1 egg yolk, room temperature
  • 100g 70% dark chocolate
  • 100g semi sweet chocolate chips
